About Us

The ACM Coaching & Career Prep platform is dedicated to providing a comprehensive AI-driven solution tailored specifically for ACM student members in the Computer Science department. Our primary objective is to prepare students for the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) while enhancing their readiness for the tech job market.

Founded by senior design team 11 (Yen Duong, Derek Papierski, and Marfn Mlakha), our platform leverages cutting-edge AI technology to align academic training with industry expectations, providing customized learning paths, real-time feedback, and comprehensive support structures.
